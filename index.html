About Us

Shrirama Dental Care is a trusted dental clinic located in Semmancheri on the bustling OMR (Rajiv Gandhi Salai), Chennai. Led by Dr. Sonal, the clinic has built a strong reputation for delivering compassionate, patient-first dental care with a gentle touch. With a stellar 4.9-star rating from over 156 satisfied patients, we are proud to be one of the most loved dental practices in the neighborhood.

From routine check-ups and cavity fillings to advanced procedures like root canals and wisdom tooth extractions, our clinic offers comprehensive dental services in a calm and reassuring environment. Dr. Sonal takes the time to understand each patient's concerns, explains every step of the treatment, and ensures minimal discomfort throughout. Whether you are visiting for the first time or have been a long-term patient, you will always feel at home at Shrirama Dental Care.
